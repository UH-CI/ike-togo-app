To impersonate another user, generate a token for that user via the CLI:
./auth-tokens-create -x username
Copy the token and paste it in the form below. You may need to reload/flush your cached data.

To get back to being yourself:
./auth-tokens-create -u your_username
(for your CLI) and log out and log back in again (for the site).
