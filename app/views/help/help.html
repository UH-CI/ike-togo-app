Chrome and Firefox.

Bring up `Ike ToGo in your browser. You may see briefly see this page:



Once forwarded, you may also see this:



This asks you if it is ok to get your information (first name, last name, and email) from the UH directory (LDAP). If you click "Approve" you will continue to the login page. If you click "Deny" you will not be allowed to log in to the system.

Assuming you select one of the "approve" options, you will reach the login page.



Enter your UH username and password here and click the 'Sign in' button. After logging in, you will be taken to the "My Data" page:


The "My Data" page is where you are taken immediately after logging in. You can also get there by clicking the "My Data" tab in the sidebar on the left hand side. This page shows all the data in your home directory. These are files to which you have full access, you can move, rename, copy, and delete files that are located here.

The "Team Data Preview" page is accessed by clicking on the tab of the same name in the sidebar on the left side of the window. This area allows you to browse and download the files of your team-mates in the `Ike Wai project. You will not be able to alter the files in this directory, it only allows you to see everyone else's data.

The sidebar can be hidden by clicking the small icon consisting of 4 horizontal lines next to the `Ike logo in the upper left corner.



At first, the sidebar looks like this:



Once minimized, it changes to this:



If the sidebar is minimized and you want to show it in full again, click the same sidebar icon, now located directly in the upper left corner.

Note that the sidebar is automatically hidden when the window is made too small. However, when the narrowness of the window triggers this, the sidebar gets moved to the top of screen in a minimized state.



If you click the sidebar icon, it will expand to look like this:




Click on the "My Data" tab in the side bar. This will bring you to the "My Data" page. Click on the "Upload File" button in the upper left corner, under the "My Data" header.



This brings up the upload files box:



You can drag your files and folders into this box, or click in the shaded area to bring up your computer's file manager system to select the files to upload. As files are added, you will see them listed:



Once all your files are included, click the "Upload" button in the bottom right corner of the box and your files will be uploaded to your directory. Progress for larger files will show a percentage of completion until finished.

On the "My Data" page, first go in to the directory in which you want your new folder to be located. Next, click on the "Create folder" button that is under the "My Data" header in the upper left corner.



This will bring up the following box in which you will enter the name of the new folder and click the "Create" button.


To rename a folder, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target folder. This brings up a dropdown menu with all folder actions.



Click the "Rename" action and a new window will appear in which you can enter the new name of the folder.



When finished, click the "Save" button.

To move a folder, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target folder. This brings up a dropdown menu with all folder actions.



Click on the "Move" action and a window will appear.



Click the "Select New Location" button and then navigate to the intended location using the resulting window.



Once the correct location is selected, click the "Use selected location" button.

To copy a folder, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target folder. This brings up a dropdown menu with all folder actions.



Click on the "Copy" action and a window will appear.



Click the "Select New Location" button and then navigate to the intended location using the resulting window.



Once the correct location is selected, click the "Use selected location" button.

To delete a folder, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target folder. This brings up a dropdown menu with all folder actions.



Click on the "Delete" action. You will then see a delete confirmation dialog box. To confirm the delete action, click the "delete" button.


To rename a file, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target file. This brings up a dropdown menu with all file actions.



Click the "Rename" action and a new window will appear in which you can enter the new name.



When finished, click the "Save" button.

To move a file, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target file. This brings up a dropdown menu with all file actions.



Click on the "Move" action and a window will appear.



Click the "Select New Location" button and then navigate to the intended location using the resulting window.



Once the correct location is selected, click the "Use selected location" button.

To copy a file, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target file. This brings up a dropdown menu with all file actions.



Click on the "Copy" action and a window will appear.



Click the "Select New Location" button and then navigate to the intended location using the resulting window.



Once the correct location is selected, click the "Use selected location" button.

To delete a file, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target file. This brings up a dropdown menu with all file actions.



Click on the "Delete" action. You will then see a delete confirmation dialog box. To confirm the delete action, click the "delete" button.


To download a file, click on the "Actions" menu to the right of the target file. This brings up a dropdown menu with all file actions.



Click on the "Download" action and the download will start immediately.

To delete multiple files at once, the files all need to be in the same directory. Check the box on the left side next to each file you want to delete. At the top of the files section of the page, there is an Actions menu. Click "Actions" to see the menu.



Click the "Delete" menu item. You will be prompted to confirm that your really want to delete the files.
